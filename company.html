Our story

Founded in 2018

AETHER began with a small team of engineers and designers who believed technology had become disposable. We set out to build objects worth keeping — engineered to a standard, not a price.

Today we ship to 42 countries, hold over 900 patents, and remain independently led from our studio and foundry.

Sustainability

Engineered to last, designed to return

Our enclosures use 100% recycled aluminium, our packaging is plastic-free, and every AETHER device is carbon-neutral from foundry to doorstep. Trade in any device and we'll recycle it responsibly — or refurbish it for a second life.
