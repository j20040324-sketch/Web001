1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve efficiency, and to provide reporting information. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage and SDKs.

2. Why We Use Them

We use cookies to operate and secure our website, remember your preferences, understand how our site is used, and—where you consent—to personalise content and measure advertising.

3. Types of Cookies

  • Strictly necessary — required for the website to function; cannot be switched off.
  • Performance / analytics — help us understand and improve how the site is used.
  • Functional — remember choices such as language and region.
  • Targeting / advertising — used to deliver and measure relevant advertising.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties that provide services on our behalf, such as analytics and advertising platforms. These parties may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage non-essential cookies at any time through our cookie settings banner. You can also control cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
